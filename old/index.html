ABOUT Books4u

Not getting desired books on college library or from your friends for semester?? Or you want to sell your used books but not getting buyers, have you ever came across such a situation?? "Books4u" is the solution. "Books4u" is a digital platform which helps you to buy or sell books as per your requirement.

What you need to do??? - Just follow these steps and get benefits of our service.

• We will find suitable buyers / seller of the book for you.
